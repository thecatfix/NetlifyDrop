ETF Data Categories & Use Cases

Core ETF Operational Data

  • Creation/Redemption Mechanisms: Real-time basket composition and authorized participant flows
  • Fund Flows: Daily net flows driven by ETF creation and redemption processes
  • NAV Calculations: Intraday and end-of-day net asset value computations
  • Pricing Data: Real-time ETF pricing, spreads, and liquidity metrics
  • Portfolio Holdings: Underlying asset composition and weightings

Alternative Data for ETF Strategy

  • Consumer Analytics: Transaction data, spending patterns, and retail behavior
  • Supply Chain Intelligence: Trade flows, shipping data, and logistics analytics
  • ESG & Sustainability: Environmental, social, and governance metrics
  • Sector-Specific Data: Healthcare trends, energy flows, technology adoption
  • Macroeconomic Indicators: Employment, inflation, and economic sentiment data

AI & Analytics Applications

  • Predictive Modeling: ETF performance forecasting using alternative datasets
  • Risk Management: Real-time portfolio risk assessment and stress testing
  • Market Making: Automated pricing and liquidity provision algorithms
  • Theme Detection: Identifying emerging investment trends and opportunities

ETF Ecosystem Infrastructure

Key Market Participants

Authorized Participants

Large financial institutions facilitating ETF creation and redemption with underlying baskets

Market Makers

Entities providing liquidity through continuous bid-ask quotes on exchanges

Custodians

Safekeeping assets and processing creation/redemption settlement operations

Transfer Agents

Managing shareholder records and processing ETF share transactions

Data Flow Architecture

  • NSCC Integration: National Securities Clearing Corporation for basket dissemination
  • IOPV Calculation: Intraday Indicative Optimized Portfolio Value streaming
  • Exchange Connectivity: Real-time market data and trading infrastructure
  • Regulatory Reporting: SEC, FINRA, and CFTC compliance data flows
